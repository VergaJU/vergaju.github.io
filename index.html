About me

I'm a data scientist and computational biologist working at the intersection of AI and biomedical research. My work focuses on leveraging data-driven approaches to decode complex biological systems and accelerate therapeutic discovery. Lately, I've been developing AI agents designed to boost scientific research, integrating techniques from machine learning, single-cell analysis, and systems immunology. I specialize in analyzing single-cell and spatial transcriptomics/proteomics data, with a strong focus on immuno-oncology and disease progression. I’m passionate about building tools and models that make biological complexity actionable — bridging the gap between data and discovery in the life sciences.
